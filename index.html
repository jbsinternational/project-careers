What is Cognitive Support Technology?

Cognitive Support Technology is the use of a device, software ("app"), or other technology-based tools to assist with tasks typically handled by the brain. This includes technology to support memory, organization, navigating, and much more. In Project Career, we are using Cognitive Support Technology (CST) to help college students who have experienced a Traumatic Brain Injury improve their academic performance.

Research Article Reviews Five Case Studies

An article about Project Career published in the the journal NeuroRehabilitation includes case studies on five Project Career participants. Authored by Amanda Nardone, Elaine Sampson, Callista Stauffer, et al., this article includes a set of qualitative analyses for each individual's case notes.
